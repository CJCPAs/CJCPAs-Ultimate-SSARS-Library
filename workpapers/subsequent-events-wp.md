## Background

### Types of Subsequent Events (ASC 855)

| Type | Description | Treatment |
|------|-------------|-----------|
| **Type I - Recognized** | Events providing evidence of conditions that existed at balance sheet date | Adjust financial statements |
| **Type II - Non-recognized** | Events providing evidence of conditions that arose after balance sheet date | Disclose if material |
