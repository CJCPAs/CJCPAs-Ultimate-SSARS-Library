## Instructions

**Evaluation Period:** One year from the date the financial statements are issued (or available to be issued).

**Management's Responsibility:** Evaluate going concern at each reporting date.

**Accountant's Responsibility:**
- **Compilation:** Address known going concern issues
- **Review:** Inquire about going concern; evaluate management's assessment

```

  ☐ Adding emphasis of matter paragraph (optional)

  ☐ Adverse (pervasive departure)
```

### 6.2 Emphasis of Matter Paragraph (If Used)

```
Emphasis of Matter

As discussed in Note ___ to the financial statements, the Company

[conclusion/report] is not modified with respect to this matter.
```
