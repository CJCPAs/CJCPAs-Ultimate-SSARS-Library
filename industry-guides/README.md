# Industry-Specific SSARS Guidance

## Overview

This section provides tailored guidance for performing SSARS engagements in specific industries. Each industry has unique accounting considerations, common issues, and specific areas that require special attention during compilation and review engagements.

---

## Available Industry Guides

| Industry | Guide | Key Considerations |
|----------|-------|-------------------|
| [Healthcare](healthcare-ssars-guide.md) | Medical practices, clinics, healthcare organizations | Revenue recognition, third-party payers, regulatory compliance |
| [Nonprofits](nonprofit-ssars-guide.md) | Charities, foundations, associations | Net asset classification, contributions, functional expenses |
| [Real Estate](real-estate-ssars-guide.md) | Property management, developers, REITs | Revenue timing, property valuation, lease accounting |
| [Construction](construction-ssars-guide.md) | Contractors, builders, specialty trades | Percentage-of-completion, retainage, job costing |
| [Restaurants & Hospitality](restaurant-ssars-guide.md) | Restaurants, hotels, food service | Inventory, tips/gratuities, franchise arrangements |
| [Professional Services](professional-services-ssars-guide.md) | Law firms, accounting firms, consultants | WIP, revenue recognition, partner compensation |
| [Manufacturing](manufacturing-ssars-guide.md) | Producers, fabricators, processors | Inventory costing, overhead allocation, WIP |
| [Retail](retail-ssars-guide.md) | Stores, e-commerce, distributors | Inventory valuation, shrinkage, gift cards |

---

## How to Use These Guides

1. **Identify the client's industry** - Select the appropriate guide(s)
2. **Review industry-specific considerations** - Understand unique issues
3. **Apply to your engagement** - Incorporate into planning and execution
4. **Reference common deficiencies** - Avoid industry-specific pitfalls
5. **Use industry ratios** - For analytical procedures in reviews

---

## Cross-Industry Considerations

Some considerations apply across multiple industries:

| Consideration | Applicable Industries |
|--------------|----------------------|
| Related party transactions | All |
| Going concern assessment | All |
| Revenue recognition timing | Most |
| Inventory valuation | Manufacturing, Retail, Construction, Restaurants |
| Regulatory compliance | Healthcare, Nonprofits |
| Lease accounting | Real Estate, Retail, Restaurants |
| Long-term contracts | Construction, Professional Services |

## Industry-Specific Analytical Procedures

Each guide includes recommended analytical procedures tailored to that industry's key performance indicators and financial statement areas.

*These guides supplement, but do not replace, the standard AR-C requirements. Always follow the applicable AR-C sections in conjunction with industry-specific guidance.*
