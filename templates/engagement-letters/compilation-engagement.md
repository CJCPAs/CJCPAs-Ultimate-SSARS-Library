# Compilation Engagement Letter Template (AR-C 80)

## Standard Compilation Engagement Letter

---

**[FIRM LETTERHEAD]**

[Date]

[Client Name]
[Client Title]
[Company Name]
[Address]
[City, State ZIP]

Dear [Client Name]:

### Engagement Purpose

This letter confirms our understanding of the terms and objectives of our engagement and the nature and limitations of the services we will provide.

### Services to Be Performed

You have requested that we perform a compilation engagement on the financial statements of **[Company Name]**, which comprise the [balance sheet/statement of financial position] as of **[Date]** and the related statements of [income/operations], [changes in stockholders' equity/changes in members' capital], and [cash flows] for the [year/period] then ended, and the related notes to the financial statements.

We will compile the financial statements in accordance with **[accounting principles generally accepted in the United States of America (U.S. GAAP) / the income tax basis of accounting / the cash basis of accounting / other: specify]**.

### Our Responsibilities

We will conduct our compilation engagement in accordance with Statements on Standards for Accounting and Review Services (SSARS) promulgated by the Accounting and Review Services Committee of the AICPA. The objective of a compilation engagement is to apply accounting and financial reporting expertise to assist management in the presentation of financial statements without undertaking to obtain or provide any assurance that there are no material modifications that should be made to the financial statements.

**We will:**
- Apply our accounting and financial reporting expertise to assist you in the presentation of the financial statements
- Read the financial statements and consider whether they appear to be appropriate in form and free from obvious material errors
- Issue a compilation report on the financial statements

**We will not:**
- Audit or review the financial statements
- Obtain or provide any assurance that there are no material modifications that should be made to the financial statements
- Verify the accuracy or completeness of information you provide
- Gather evidence for the purpose of expressing an opinion or a conclusion

### Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:

1. **Preparation and fair presentation** of the financial statements in accordance with **[applicable framework]**
2. **Designing, implementing, and maintaining internal control** relevant to the preparation and fair presentation of financial statements that are free from material misstatement, whether due to fraud or error
3. **Preventing and detecting fraud**
4. **Identifying and ensuring compliance** with the laws and regulations applicable to the company's activities
5. **Making all management decisions and performing all management functions**
6. **Designating individuals with suitable skill, knowledge, or experience** to oversee any nonattest services we may provide

You are also responsible for providing us with:
- Access to all information of which you are aware that is relevant to the preparation and fair presentation of the financial statements
- Additional information we may request for the purpose of the compilation
- Unrestricted access to persons within the entity of whom we determine it is necessary to make inquiries

### Disclosure of Financial Statements

**[Choose appropriate option:]**

**Option A - Full Disclosures:**
The financial statements will include all disclosures required by **[applicable framework]**.

**Option B - Substantially All Disclosures Omitted:**
You have informed us that the financial statements will omit substantially all disclosures **[and the statement of cash flows]** required by **[applicable framework]**. We understand that if the omitted disclosures **[and statement of cash flows]** were included in the financial statements, they might influence the user's conclusions about the company's financial position, results of operations, and cash flows.

### Compilation Report

We will issue a compilation report on the financial statements. Our report will indicate that we did not audit or review the financial statements and that, accordingly, we do not express an opinion, a conclusion, nor provide any form of assurance on them.

### Independence

**[Choose appropriate option:]**

**Option A - Independent:**
We are independent with respect to **[Company Name]** within the meaning of the AICPA Code of Professional Conduct.

**Option B - Not Independent:**
We are not independent with respect to **[Company Name]**. Our compilation report will disclose that we are not independent. We are not required to, and will not, disclose the reason for our lack of independence.

### Known Departures

If we become aware of a departure from **[applicable framework]**, we will request that you revise the financial statements. If you do not revise the financial statements, we will modify our report to disclose the departure.

### Fees

Our fee for this engagement will be **[$XXX / based on our standard hourly rates / as agreed]**. This fee is based on anticipated cooperation from your personnel and the assumption that unexpected circumstances will not be encountered during the engagement.

**[Optional fee provisions:]**
- Invoices are due upon presentation
- A finance charge of X% per month will be assessed on any unpaid balance after 30 days
- In the event of nonpayment, you agree to pay all collection costs, including reasonable attorney's fees

### Other Terms

**[Include any additional terms such as:]**
- Limitation of liability provisions
- Dispute resolution procedures
- Record retention policies
- Use of third-party service providers
- Additional nonattest services to be performed

### Agreement

Please sign and return the attached copy of this letter to indicate your acknowledgment of, and agreement with, the arrangements for our engagement to compile the financial statements described herein, and our respective responsibilities.

We appreciate the opportunity to serve you. If you have any questions, please contact us.

Sincerely,

**[Signature]**
[CPA Name]
[Firm Name]

## Variations and Notes

### For Recurring Engagements

Add after "Services to Be Performed":

> This engagement letter will be effective for all compilation engagements performed for **[Company Name]** until it is terminated, amended, or superseded.

### For Engagements Including Nonattest Services

Add:

> In connection with this engagement, we will also provide the following nonattest services: **[list services such as bookkeeping, adjusting entries, etc.]**. You agree to:
> - Assume all management responsibilities
> - Oversee the nonattest services by designating an individual with suitable skill, knowledge, or experience
> - Evaluate the adequacy and results of the services performed
> - Accept responsibility for the results of the services

### Required Elements Checklist

Ensure your engagement letter includes:

- [ ] Objective and scope of the engagement
- [ ] Management's responsibilities
- [ ] Accountant's responsibilities
- [ ] Limitations of the engagement (no assurance)
- [ ] Financial reporting framework to be used
- [ ] Expected form and content of report
- [ ] Independence status (if not independent)
- [ ] Disclosure options (full or omitted)
- [ ] Fee arrangement
- [ ] Client signature/acknowledgment
