# Preparation Engagement Letter Template (AR-C 70)

## Standard Preparation Engagement Letter

---

**[FIRM LETTERHEAD]**

[Date]

[Client Name]
[Client Title]
[Company Name]
[Address]
[City, State ZIP]

Dear [Client Name]:

### Engagement Purpose

This letter confirms our understanding of the terms and objectives of our engagement and the nature and limitations of the services we will provide.

### Services to Be Performed

You have requested that we prepare the financial statements of **[Company Name]**, which comprise the [balance sheet/statement of financial position] as of **[Date]** and the related statements of [income/operations], [changes in stockholders' equity/changes in members' capital], and [cash flows, if applicable] for the [year/period] then ended, and the related notes to the financial statements.

We will prepare the financial statements in accordance with **[accounting principles generally accepted in the United States of America (U.S. GAAP) / the income tax basis of accounting / the cash basis of accounting / other: specify]**.

### Our Responsibilities

We will conduct our engagement in accordance with Statements on Standards for Accounting and Review Services (SSARS) promulgated by the Accounting and Review Services Committee of the AICPA. We will prepare the financial statements based on information you provide.

**Please be aware that we will not:**
- Audit or review the financial statements
- Obtain or provide any assurance that there are no material modifications that should be made to the financial statements
- Verify the accuracy or completeness of the information you provide
- Gather evidence for the purpose of expressing an opinion or a conclusion

### Legend Requirement

Each page of the financial statements will include a legend stating: **"No assurance is provided on these financial statements."** This legend is required by professional standards.

### Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:

1. **Preparation and fair presentation** of the financial statements in accordance with **[applicable framework]**
2. **Designing, implementing, and maintaining internal control** relevant to the preparation and fair presentation of the financial statements
3. **Preventing and detecting fraud**
4. **Ensuring compliance** with the laws and regulations applicable to its activities
5. **Providing accurate and complete information** necessary for the preparation of the financial statements
6. **Making all management decisions and performing all management functions**

You are also responsible for providing us with:
- Access to all information of which you are aware that is relevant to the preparation of the financial statements
- Additional information that we may request for the purpose of the engagement
- Unrestricted access to persons within the entity of whom we determine we need to inquire

### Disclosure of Financial Statements

**[Choose appropriate option:]**

**Option A - Full Disclosures:**
The financial statements will include all disclosures required by **[applicable framework]**.

**Option B - Substantially All Disclosures Omitted:**
You have informed us that the financial statements are for **[management's use only / internal use only / other: specify]** and that you do not intend to provide them to third parties. Accordingly, you have requested that we prepare financial statements that **omit substantially all disclosures required by [applicable framework]**. We understand that the financial statements may not be suitable for another purpose.

### Independence

We are not required to be independent of **[Company Name]** to perform this engagement.

### Fees

Our fee for these services will be **[$XXX / based on our standard hourly rates / as agreed]**. This fee estimate is based on anticipated cooperation from your personnel and the assumption that unexpected circumstances will not be encountered during the engagement.

**[Optional fee provisions:]**
- Invoices are due upon presentation
- A finance charge of X% per month will be assessed on any unpaid balance after 30 days
- In the event of nonpayment, you agree to pay all collection costs, including reasonable attorney's fees

### Other Terms

**[Include any additional terms such as:]**
- Limitation of liability provisions
- Dispute resolution procedures
- Record retention policies
- Use of third-party service providers

### Agreement

Please sign and return the attached copy of this letter to indicate your acknowledgment of, and agreement with, the arrangements for our engagement to prepare the financial statements described herein, and our respective responsibilities.

We appreciate the opportunity to serve you. If you have any questions, please contact us.

Sincerely,

**[Signature]**
[CPA Name]
[Firm Name]

### ACKNOWLEDGMENT AND AGREEMENT

The services and terms described in this engagement letter are in accordance with our requirements and are acceptable to us.

**[Company Name]**

## Variations and Notes

### For Recurring Engagements

Add the following paragraph after "Services to Be Performed":

> This engagement letter will be effective for all preparation engagements performed for **[Company Name]** until it is terminated, amended, or superseded. We will prepare **[monthly/quarterly/annual]** financial statements under this engagement.

### For Specific Purpose Statements

Add:

> The financial statements are intended solely for **[specify purpose]** and should not be used for any other purpose.

### Required Elements Checklist

Ensure your engagement letter includes:

- [ ] Objective and scope of the engagement
- [ ] Management's responsibilities
- [ ] Accountant's responsibilities
- [ ] Limitations of the engagement (no assurance)
- [ ] Financial reporting framework to be used
- [ ] Legend requirement disclosure
- [ ] Fee arrangement
- [ ] Client signature/acknowledgment
