# Standard Review Report Template (AR-C 90)

## Unmodified Conclusion

Use this report when:
- Financial statements include all required disclosures
- Accountant is independent of the entity (required)
- No known departures from the framework
- No scope limitations
- No emphasis of matter needed

---

## Report Template

```
                    INDEPENDENT ACCOUNTANT'S REVIEW REPORT


To the [Board of Directors / Shareholders / Partners / Members] of
[Company Name]
[City, State]


I (We) have reviewed the accompanying financial statements of [Company Name], which
comprise the [balance sheet / statement of financial position] as of [Month Day,
Year], and the related statements of income, [changes in stockholders' equity /
changes in partners' capital / changes in members' equity], and cash flows for the
[year / period] then ended, and the related notes to the financial statements.


Management's Responsibility for the Financial Statements

Management is responsible for the preparation and fair presentation of these
financial statements in accordance with accounting principles generally accepted
in the United States of America; this includes the design, implementation, and
maintenance of internal control relevant to the preparation and fair presentation
of financial statements that are free from material misstatement, whether due to
fraud or error.


Accountant's Responsibility

My (Our) responsibility is to conduct the review engagement in accordance with
Statements on Standards for Accounting and Review Services promulgated by the
Accounting and Review Services Committee of the AICPA. Those standards require
me (us) to perform procedures to obtain limited assurance as a basis for
reporting whether I am (we are) aware of any material modifications that should
be made to the financial statements for them to be in accordance with accounting
principles generally accepted in the United States of America. I (We) believe
that the results of my (our) procedures provide a reasonable basis for my (our)
conclusion.

I am (We are) required to be independent of [Company Name] and to meet my (our)
other ethical responsibilities, in accordance with the relevant ethical
requirements related to my (our) review.


Accountant's Conclusion

Based on my (our) review, I am (we are) not aware of any material modifications
that should be made to the accompanying financial statements in order for them
to be in accordance with accounting principles generally accepted in the United
States of America.


[Signature of accounting firm or accountant]

[City, State]

[Date of report]
```

## Key Elements Checklist

### Header Section
- [ ] Title: "INDEPENDENT ACCOUNTANT'S REVIEW REPORT"
- [ ] Appropriate addressee

### Introductory Paragraph
- [ ] Company name
- [ ] Financial statements identified (all four statements plus notes)
- [ ] Statement date and period covered
- [ ] Framework identified (GAAP)

### Management's Responsibility Section
- [ ] Preparation and fair presentation responsibility
- [ ] Internal control responsibility
- [ ] Free from material misstatement (fraud or error)

### Accountant's Responsibility Section
- [ ] Reference to SSARS
- [ ] Procedures to obtain limited assurance
- [ ] Belief that results provide reasonable basis
- [ ] Independence statement
- [ ] Ethical requirements statement

### Accountant's Conclusion Section
- [ ] "Based on my (our) review..."
- [ ] "Not aware of any material modifications..."
- [ ] Framework reference

### Signature Block
- [ ] Signature
- [ ] City and state
- [ ] Report date

---

## Dating Considerations

The report should be dated **no earlier than** the date on which the accountant has:
- Completed the review procedures, including analytical procedures and inquiries
- Obtained the signed management representation letter
- Made final evaluations

---

## Independence Reminder

**Independence is REQUIRED for review engagements.** If independence is impaired:
- Cannot issue a review report
- Consider stepping down to compilation
- Document the situation

---

## Differences from Compilation Report

| Element | Review Report | Compilation Report |
|---------|--------------|-------------------|
| Sections | Multiple headed sections | Single paragraph |
| Independence statement | Required in report | Only if NOT independent |
| Conclusion | Limited assurance expressed | No assurance stated |
| Management responsibility | Detailed section | Brief statement |
| Accountant responsibility | Detailed section | Brief statement |
