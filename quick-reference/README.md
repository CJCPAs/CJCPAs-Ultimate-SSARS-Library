## Key Phrases to Remember

### Preparation Legend
> "No assurance is provided on these financial statements."

### Compilation Report
> "I (We) do not express an opinion, a conclusion, nor provide any form of assurance..."

### Review Conclusion
> "Based on my (our) review, I am (we are) not aware of any material modifications..."

### Not Independent (Compilation)
> "I am (We are) not independent with respect to [Company Name]."

## Emergency Reference

### What if client needs assurance but you're not independent?
1. Cannot perform review
2. Consider stepping down to compilation
3. Disclose lack of independence in compilation report

### What if management won't sign rep letter (review)?
1. Cannot issue review report
2. Consider compilation instead
3. Document the refusal

### What if you discover a departure?
1. Propose correction to management
2. If refused: modify report
3. Compilation: describe departure
4. Review: qualified or adverse conclusion
