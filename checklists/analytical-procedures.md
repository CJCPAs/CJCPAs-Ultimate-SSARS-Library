# Analytical Procedures Checklist for Review Engagements

## Overview

Analytical procedures are **REQUIRED** for review engagements under AR-C 90. This checklist provides a comprehensive framework for performing and documenting analytical procedures.

---

## Purpose of Analytical Procedures

1. **Identify unusual relationships** that may indicate material misstatement
2. **Develop expectations** for financial statement amounts
3. **Compare expectations to recorded amounts**
4. **Investigate significant differences**
5. **Form a basis for the accountant's conclusion**

---

## Pre-Engagement Considerations

### Understanding to Develop Expectations

Before performing analytical procedures, obtain an understanding of:

- [ ] **Industry factors** - Economic conditions, regulatory environment
- [ ] **Entity operations** - Nature of business, products/services
- [ ] **Accounting policies** - Significant policies, recent changes
- [ ] **Prior period results** - Historical trends and patterns
- [ ] **Budget information** - If available, management's expectations
- [ ] **Known changes** - New business, discontinued operations, restructuring
