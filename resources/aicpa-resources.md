### Independence Rules for SSARS

| Service | Independence Required |
|---------|----------------------|
| Preparation (AR-C 70) | No |
| Compilation (AR-C 80) | No* |
| Review (AR-C 90) | **Yes** |

*If not independent, must disclose in report

### How to Stay Updated

1. **Join AICPA** - Member access to resources
2. **Subscribe to newsletters** - JofA, CPA Letter
3. **Monitor ARSC** - Exposure drafts and new pronouncements
4. **Attend training** - SSARS-focused CPE
5. **Check state boards** - State-specific requirements
6. **Review peer review alerts** - Common deficiencies

### Key Dates to Watch

- ARSC meeting dates
- Exposure draft comment periods
- New standard effective dates
- Peer review cycle deadlines
